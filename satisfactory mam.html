the storyline

you are sent

In Satisfactory you are a pioneer that is sent by a company called Ficsit.

As you build the hub you start to build machines and get upgrades.

Then you build the space elevator and complete phase one to five.

As you finish phase five you realize that you have been building a spaceship.

Finally, after a long time of work, you watch as the spaceship teleports into the mesmerizing galaxy.

how to play

you need to click create world and choose the first one as it is the most suitable for new players

then you can watch the mini tutorial when you're in orbit

then click F to go into dismantle mode

then go over to your drop pod and hold your left mouse button over it to dismantle it

then click TAB and drag the zeno basher into your hand slot on the right hand side of your inventory

then click Q to go onto your build menu

then click the hub and place it down

then click E to interact with the hub

then click the first milestone and click the button to start it

then find leaves and wood around you and click E to pick them up

then click Q to go back to the build menu and click the biomass burner

then place it down and click E to interact with it

then put the leaves and wood into the biomass burner

then click V to scan for iron

then mine some iron ore by hovering your cursor over it and click E

then click Q to go back to the build menu and click the smelter

then place it down and click E to interact with it

then click the iron ingot recipe and click the button to start it

but before you do that click Q and go to cable and connect it to the biomass burner

then connect it to your smelter as well

then put the iron ore into the smelter

then click Q to go back to the build menu and click the constructor

then place it down and click E to interact with it

then click the iron plate recipe and click the button to start it

then connect it to the smelter and put the iron ingots into the constructor

then click Q to go back to the build menu and click the storage container

then place it down and click E to interact with it

then put the iron plates into the storage container with a conveyor belt

then click Q to go back to the build menu and click the miner

then place it down and click E to interact with it

then click the iron ore recipe and click the button to start it

then connect it to the smelter

then complete hub upgrade 1

and that's the first stage of satisfactory!!!!!
